MLR 20% Market Liquidity & Resalability: Merged component (60% unit resalability + 40% market depth) — eliminates double-counting of illiquidity that existed in old RI+ML separation.
DM 15% Dynamic Momentum: PSF appreciation trend with data-derived segment multiplier — replaces hardcoded CCR 0.57 penalty with live-computed relative performance per market segment.
NTC 15% Non-linear Transit Connectivity: Logistic sigmoid decay centred at 480 m (psychological ~6 min cliff) — reflects real buyer behaviour: steep drop at 400–550 m, flat below 300 m and above 650 m.
ESQ 15% Enhanced Spatial Quality: 70% MasterPlan 2025 land use (Green/Buzz/Education/Upside/Purity) + 30% actual primary school proximity (elite SAP/autonomous schools weighted 2×).
YR 10% Yield Representation: Estimated gross rental yield = sqft × region rental PSF × 12 / price. Anchors valuation to income potential — prevents overpaying for trophy assets with no rental upside.
