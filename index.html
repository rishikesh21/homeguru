💎 GEM Score — Hidden Gem Unified Scoring Model
GEM = 0.15·NTC + 0.15·AVA + 0.16·SCH + 0.18·MLR + 0.18·NLG + 0.07·ESQ + 0.04·DM + 0.07·YR
NTC 15% MRT Walk: OSRM actual foot-routing (not straight-line). Sigmoid cliff at 480 m — projects up to 1,200 m included (TEL corridor). Score drops sharply beyond 600 m.
AVA 18% Value Alpha: PSF vs size-adjusted district peers. Value-trap guard: >25% below peers → 40% penalty.
SCH 18% Schools: Primary schools within 1 km (Phase 2B radius). 17 elite SAP/gifted schools weighted 2×. Elite school nearby = $200–500K premium.
MLR 13% Liquidity: Bedroom size + buyer pool + market activity. 3BR★ ≥1,150 sqft = deepest exit market.
NLG 12% New Launch Gap: PSF discount vs comparable 99yr new launches. Freehold at $1,400 vs new at $2,100 = $700/sqft saved.
ESQ 10% Land Use: MasterPlan 2025 — Green Space, Urban Buzz, WHITE zones upside, Purity from industrial noise.
DM 6% Momentum: PSF trend with segment multiplier. Tiebreaker between similar projects.
YR 3% Yield: Est. gross rental yield. Low weight for owner-occupiers; guards against trophy-asset traps.
