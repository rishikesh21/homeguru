PRISM = 0.25·AVA + 0.18·MLR + 0.12·DM + 0.15·NTC + 0.13·ESQ + 0.05·YR + 0.12·NLG
AVA 25% Adjusted Value Alpha: PSF vs size-band-adjusted district peers (150 sqft bands) — no more comparing 474 sqft studios to 1,150 sqft 3BRs. Value trap guard: >25% below peers penalised 40% (structural issues likely).
MLR 18% Market Liquidity & Resalability: Merged (60% bedroom quality + buyer pool + 40% market depth). Bedroom scoring: 3BR★ ≥1,150 sqft → 100 pts, 3BR Compact ≥1,050 → 90, 2BR ≥1,000 → 75. HDB upgrader demand is deepest for 3BR — the widest resale market.
DM 12% Dynamic Momentum: PSF appreciation trend with data-derived segment multiplier — replaces hardcoded CCR 0.57 penalty with live-computed relative performance per market segment.
NTC 15% Non-linear Transit Connectivity: Logistic sigmoid decay centred at 480 m (psychological ~6 min cliff) — reflects real buyer behaviour: steep drop at 400–550 m, flat below 300 m and above 650 m.
ESQ 13% Enhanced Spatial Quality: 70% MasterPlan 2025 land use (Green/Buzz/Education/Upside/Purity) + 30% actual primary school proximity (elite SAP/autonomous schools weighted 2×).
YR 5% Yield Representation: Estimated gross rental yield = sqft × region rental PSF × 12 / price. Anchors valuation to income potential — prevents overpaying for trophy assets with no rental upside.
NLG 12% New Launch Gap: PSF discount vs comparable new 99yr launches in same segment. The #1 realtor pitch: freehold resale at $1,400 PSF when new launch is $2,100 PSF = $700/sqft savings + freehold tenure. Buyers can tangibly see the value — the fastest-converting listing.

🏛️ Expert Analysis: Freehold Resale vs 99yr New Launch
🏠 Freehold Resale — The Case FOR
✅ Land ownership forever — no lease erosion, no 99yr decay risk
✅ 20–35% cheaper PSF vs comparable new 99yr launches in same area
✅ Move in immediately — no 3–4 year TOP wait; lower ABSD quantum
✅ What you see is what you get — walk the actual unit before committing
✅ En-bloc / redevelopment upside — older freehold on large land = jackpot scenario
✅ Lower mortgage quantum — same location, same size, smaller loan, lower monthly payment
✅ Survived a property cycle — price history gives realistic baseline
⚠️ Older fittings and facilities — budget S$50–100K for renovation
🏗️ New 99yr Launch — The Case AGAINST (for matrimonial home)
❌ Pay developer premium PSF — marketing, showflat costs priced in
❌ Tenure decay after ~30 years — resale becomes progressively harder at 70yr mark
❌ 3–4 year wait — pay mortgage + rent simultaneously during construction
❌ Cannot view the actual unit — showflat is never exactly your floor/stack
❌ Higher quantum → higher stamp duty — BSD scales with price
❌ Investor-heavy buyer pool — at resale, you compete with new launches in same area
✅ Brand new facilities, developer warranty, modern layout
