💎 GEM Score — Hidden Gem Unified Scoring Model
GEM = 0.20·NTC + 0.18·AVA + 0.18·SCH + 0.13·MLR + 0.12·NLG + 0.10·ESQ + 0.06·DM + 0.03·YR
NTC 20% MRT Walk (actual routing): OSRM foot-routing from LTA exit coordinates — not a straight-line estimate. Logistic sigmoid cliff at 480 m (~6 min) reflects real buyer drop-off. Only projects within 800 m actual walk are included.
AVA 18% Adjusted Value Alpha: PSF vs size-band-adjusted district peers (150 sqft bands). Value trap guard: >25% below peers penalised 40%. Are you buying cheap for a reason, or genuinely undervalued?
SCH 18% School Proximity: Primary schools within 1 km (Phase 2B registration radius). 17 elite SAP/autonomous/gifted schools weighted 2×. Elite school within 1 km = $200–500K resale premium.
MLR 13% Market Liquidity & Resalability: Merged score (bedroom quality + buyer pool depth + market activity). 3BR★ ≥1,150 sqft → 100 pts — deepest HDB upgrader demand, widest exit market.
NLG 12% New Launch Gap: PSF discount vs comparable new 99yr launches in the same segment. Freehold resale at $1,400 PSF when new launch is $2,100 PSF = $700/sqft saved + freehold tenure.
ESQ 10% Land Use Quality: MasterPlan 2025 proximity analysis — Green Space (500 m), Urban Buzz (300 m), Future Upside / WHITE zones (800 m), Purity from industrial nuisance (500 m).
DM 6% Dynamic Momentum: PSF appreciation trend with data-derived segment multiplier. Tiebreaker for otherwise similar projects.
YR 3% Yield Anchor: Estimated gross rental yield. Minimal weight for owner-occupiers; prevents pure trophy-asset traps.
EN-BLOC▲ Freehold + top-quartile WHITE/C&R density within 800 m (p75 threshold) — strongest en-bloc catalyst signal.

🏠 Freehold Resale — The Case FOR
✅ Land ownership forever — no lease erosion, no 99yr decay risk
✅ 20–35% cheaper PSF vs comparable new 99yr launches in same area
✅ Move in immediately — no 3–4 year TOP wait; lower ABSD quantum
✅ What you see is what you get — walk the actual unit before committing
✅ En-bloc / redevelopment upside — older freehold on large land = jackpot scenario
✅ Lower mortgage quantum — same location, same size, smaller loan, lower monthly payment
✅ Survived a property cycle — price history gives realistic baseline
⚠️ Older fittings and facilities — budget S$50–100K for renovation
🏗️ New 99yr Launch — The Case AGAINST (for matrimonial home)
❌ Pay developer premium PSF — marketing, showflat costs priced in
❌ Tenure decay after ~30 years — resale becomes progressively harder at 70yr mark
❌ 3–4 year wait — pay mortgage + rent simultaneously during construction
❌ Cannot view the actual unit — showflat is never exactly your floor/stack
❌ Higher quantum → higher stamp duty — BSD scales with price
❌ Investor-heavy buyer pool — at resale, you compete with new launches in same area
✅ Brand new facilities, developer warranty, modern layout
